## Who this is for

This handbook is for **professionals who want Claude to make their day-to-day work measurably better**, whether you write, analyze, plan, build, support customers, or manage teams. You don't need to be technical. You just need real work to get done.

By the end you'll be able to:

- Choose the right Claude model and interface for each task.
- Write prompts that get the result you want on the first or second try.
- Turn one-off chats into **repeatable workflows** your whole team can reuse.
- Use Projects, Artifacts, files, Skills, and integrations to go beyond simple Q&A.
- Work with Claude responsibly, knowing its limits and protecting your data.
